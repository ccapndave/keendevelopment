This was an Actionscript 3.0 pinball simulator built for Nurofen to promote the release of their new ‘Nurofen Express’ pills. The project makes use of real-time physics and has a very tidy object-oriented design allowing the addition of new tables and objects.  

I incorporated many features from classic pinball games that I loved, such as Pinball Dreams and Pinball Fantasies including bonus multipliers, extra balls, multiball, jackpots and much more.
